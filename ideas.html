IDEA/THESIS

In this work, I will explore the bridge between virtual and real-world experiences. Drawing inspiration from early video games featuring low-poly models and low-fi graphics, I investigate the ways in which digital and physical realms influence and reshape one another.

Virtual reality transforms imagination into reality by immersing individuals in fantastical environments and challenging them to navigate and comprehend their surroundings. This process accentuates the unique ability of virtual reality to engage players in dynamic and thought-provoking experiences. Conversely, I aim to bring a digital character into materiality, allowing it to modify our perception of reality.

The sculpture reveals the true emotions and characteristics in digital platforms including social media and video games. The psychedelic aesthetics infuse it with a surreal quality within a gallery space. The true extent of my inner greed becomes strangely distorted when contrasted with reality, which can be repressive and unreal.





Cryptic Synthesis, 2023
Dimension: 6*5*1ft
Resin, Foam, Acrylic, and Wood
