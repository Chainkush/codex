FORM/MATERIAL

The sculpture will be 6 feet tall and 2 feet wide, emphasizing its presence and connection to human proportions in reality. The low poly treatment will accentuate the digital origins of the piece. The use of highly saturated colors in psychedelic patterns will evoke a hallucinatory experience. This aesthetic choice contributes to the overall disorienting and immersive atmosphere. The choice of 3D-printing technology is crucial for realizing its transition from virtual space to real space.

A high-quality resin material will allow for durability and a smooth surface. The choice of airbrushing acrylic paint is to assure proper adhesion to the surface and to recreate the low-fi texture of early video games.
